Projects

Battle For The Realm

Battle for the Realm was the first project I worked on. It is the card game War, where you and an opponent flip cards to see who has the higher card and wins the battle. Once one player has won the majority of the hands, the game is over.

Community Cookbook

The Community Cookbook was an idea I had for my second project. I built an app where users could come together and share all of their favorite recipes, as well as seeing what recipes other users have added. There are multiple categories to choose from including breakfast, soups/salads, one pot meals, and more.

Youtorial

Youtorial was the third project I worked on at GA. This was a group project that we built using Python/Django. Youtorial is a user generated tutorial site. Users are able to create tutorials on one of the current categories or create their own new one. The tutorials can be written instructions, a video, or both. Users are able to save tutorials they like to be completed later and they can rate the quality of the tutorials. Once finished, they go into the completed tutorials section on their profile page.

What Do You Know?

What Do You Know? was my final project at GA. It is a trivia app I made using React.js and the OpenTrivia API. You can select trivia from a number of different categories and difficulty, as well as selecting the number of questions to answer. Once finished, you will see how many questions you answered correctly or incorrectly, as well as the answers. It keeps a running total of your lifetime correct and incorrect answers.

About
